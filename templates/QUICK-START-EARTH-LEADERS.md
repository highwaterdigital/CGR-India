## â“ FAQ

**Q: Will my existing data work?**  
A: Yes! All existing data works perfectly with no changes needed.

**Q: Can I use multiple filters at once?**  
A: Absolutely! Combine search, district, year, and organization filters.

**Q: How do I reset everything?**  
A: Click "Clear All" in the active filters section, or click "Reset All Filters" in the no-results message.

**Q: Does export include filtered results only?**  
A: Yes! Export only includes leaders currently visible based on your filters.

**Q: Can I sort by multiple columns?**  
A: Sort by one column at a time. Secondary sorting is automatically by name.

**Q: How many leaders load at once?**  
A: 100 leaders per batch. Click "Load More" to load the next 100.
